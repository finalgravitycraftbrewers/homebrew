About Us

Final Gravity is an Atlanta, GA based club that promotes homebrewing and appreciation of beer for beginners and experts alike. We meet every first Wednesday of the month at 7:30 PM to discuss the many aspects of home brewing. Through technical discussions, group brewing, and group experiments, we strive to share knowledge and obtain the skills to become better brewers. The club was started by Bob Sandage in 2005 and has been active in the Little 5 Points area ever since.

Our primary communication to the club is conducted via Google Groups: Final-Gravity. We also have a Facebook group forum for discussion at Final Gravity Craftbrewers of Atlanta.

Meetings

Meetings are generally held the first Wednesday of each month at 7:30pm. We meet at different locations around the Little 5 Points area based on availability and send a reminder via the Google Group to notify everyone of the location. Our standing agenda includes (1) discussion of past and upcoming events, (2) presentation and evaluation of homebrews and BJCP-style judging feedback, and (3) technical presentations on topics that are intended to further the knowledge and brewing proficiency of club members.

Group Brews

We conduct several group brew events on our custom-built club brew stand and control panel with 50 gallon kettles throughout the year. We collectively determine a style and recipe for each event. Our next group brew is June, 25, 2016. Please contact the club or register with our Google Group to learn more details.

Dues

Club dues are $35 per calendar year. Club dues are used as seed money for this website, club-hosted competitions, barrels for group brews, tasting glasses, and shirts. You do not have to be a member to attend a club meeting.
